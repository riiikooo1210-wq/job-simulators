## Prompt

Generate a 16:9 side-view gameplay image for a family-friendly console puzzle-platformer tutorial room.

The image should look like a small playable level slice, not a poster or concept-art splash. Show a simple readable path from left to right:

- On the far left, a safe start platform where a beginner player could stand and look around.
- Near the first gap, one clearly interactive Wind Bud flower. It should not look like background decoration.
- From the Wind Bud, show a short wind gust cone flowing clearly toward the right.
- On the right, show a reachable landing platform that the gust would carry the player toward.
- After the landing, show a small optional coin trail as a reward path, but keep it secondary so it does not distract from the Wind Bud lesson.

The Wind Bud should read as usable: make it slightly open, with a simple stem, a terracotta flower head, a few drifting leaves, and wind direction shapes coming out of it. The gust should be visible as a pale blue-green cone or curved wind shape moving from the Wind Bud toward the landing platform. Leave clean empty space around the start platform, Wind Bud, gust cone, landing platform, and coins so annotation hotspots can be placed over them later.

Do not include any UI, text labels, captions, button shapes, logos, dialogue, characters, HUD, health bars, controller prompts, or readable signs. This should be a clean game-level image that can sit inside a simulator as an interactive map.

Art style:
Clean 2D game mockup / flat editorial game illustration. Use flat painting only. Every object should have one solid base color and, if needed, one simple flat shadow shape. Use thin black outlines around platforms, the Wind Bud, coins, gust shapes, and major environment edges. The outlines should be clean and confident, not sketchy, not thick comic-book ink.

Use this exact color palette:

- Background sky / empty space: #EFE8D2
- Main platforms / stone or wood surfaces: #CDBF94
- Ground, grass, leaves, and plant shapes: #5F7F62
- Dark plant stems and deep accents: #3F605C
- Wind Bud flower accent: #C75448
- Gust cone / wind path fill: #A8C7BE
- Coin fill: #E8DCC8
- Thin black outlines: #111111

The final image should be simple, readable, and beginner-friendly. The player should immediately understand: stand on the left, notice the Wind Bud, trigger the wind, ride the gust to the right platform, then collect optional coins.

Avoid photorealism, 3D rendering, anime style, pixel art, painterly brush strokes, gradients, airbrushing, bloom, glow effects, soft lighting, lens flare, realistic textures, heavy shadows, busy backgrounds, and readable text.
