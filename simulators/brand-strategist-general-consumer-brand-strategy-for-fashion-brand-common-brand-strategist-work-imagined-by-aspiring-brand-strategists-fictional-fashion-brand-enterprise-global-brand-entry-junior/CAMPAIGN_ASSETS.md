# Campaign Studio Asset Prompts

These assets power the `final_note` 4:5 vertical static post builder. Each prompt below is standalone and repeats the required art style and palette.

## Strategic Key

Correct background:
`deep_sage_motion`

Correct headline/support pair:
`proof_led_ease` + `fabric_fit_range`

Acceptable aligned stickers:
`fabric_proof`, `fit_lines`, `day_to_night`, `craft_tag`

Distractor stickers:
`meme_megaphone`, `discount_burst`, `generic_crown`
